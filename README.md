## Repository Description  
This repository contains my coursework for *ITCS 2530 – C++ Programming Assignments*. Alongside my academic work, it also highlights one of my personal interests and the path that led me into the world of technology.

My interest in tech began in 2019 when I started my own podcast and wanted to build a website to support it. That project introduced me to the creative and technical possibilities of software, and it sparked a long-term curiosity about how technology works behind the scenes.

In 2024, I decided to take that interest more seriously by enrolling in a Cloud Engineering and DevOps program, which I completed in 2025. The program gave me valuable hands-on experience, but it also made me realize the importance of strong computer science fundamentals. That realization ultimately led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science.

Outside of academics and tech, I enjoy longboarding and skateboarding. Cruising through the city or practicing at local skateparks has taught me lessons that carry over into my approach to programming: how to fall, reassess, and try again. Every fall becomes an opportunity to grow, building patience, adaptability, and persistence—qualities that support both my personal interests and my development as a programmer.
